What the heck is this?

This is Jiirist, a web application for public shareable lists.

Every time you enter Jiirist, we create an public list for you that you can share with anyone. You can lock it down if you want to, but you won't be able to edit it later on.

For what?

Jiirist is convenient for creating grocery lists, simple to-dos or even poetry. Or, anything really.

Simply write a title and list the things you want to remember or share.

Hey, I wanna be cool and share my list with friends!

You can share your public list via email, Twitter or Facebook by clicking Share.

Who's the idiot who made this?

Jiirist was created by @slermukka, @tommikaikkonen and @mikipernu from Jiiri.com. We hate writing on small paper notes as they always get lost, so we decided to code a small paper scrap for you to play around with.

We love feedback, and we want to make our app better. Tweet your feedback to @tommikaikkonen. Thanks a bunch!
